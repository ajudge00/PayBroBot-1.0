Fake PayPal
- new_account
	user, password (encrypted), account_num (encrypted)
	10000 coins when account created
- login
	user, password, 2fa
- create_pocket
	balance can be split up into up to 4 pockets
- new_transfer
	can transfer money to other users
- list_transfers
	can see a history of in- and outgoing transfers
- see_dough
	can see the current balance, split up by pockets, if any
- get_notifications
	an option to turn on, you get a message whenever someone transfers money to your account
- get_stats
	you can generate a diagram of your spendings/earnings by pockets and have it sent to you as an image
